INTRODUCTION					*ft-help_fold-introduction*

*ft-help_fold* is a Vim plugin to provide the setting for folding of vim help.

This plugin folds
- the area that following by === as level 1
- the area that following by --- as level 2
- helptag(*word*) and following as level 3


Requirements:
- Vim 7.3 or later

Latest version:
https://github.com/thinca/vim-ft-help_fold
